One-Player Tic Tac Toe

A classic game for one player
How to Play
  • In this Dynamic version, As the 7th mark is placed, the oldest mark gets removed.
  • In order to avoid the draw situation we have to keep 3 spaces empty always.
  • The small number at the corner of each cell shows the move order.
  • Player "O" plays first..!!
  • Always mind your next step before marking any cell. Check which of your mark is the oldest.
